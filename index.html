About me

I was born in Fayetteville, North Carolina on March 27th, 2000. I enjoy playing videogames, reading and watching movies with my friends. I was a military brat growing up and frequently moved, living in Texas and Georgia.

In March of 2020, I decided to enlist into the United States Air Force. This was a particularly interesting time. The COVID epidemic happened and resulted in a very unique experience. We were creating masks out of t-shirts, had all military events cancelled, and were restricted to staying on the military base for almost eight months. In November of 2021, I was sent to Phoenix, Arizona as my first duty station I enjoy living here, I have always preferred the heat over colder climates and the city always has things to do. I work as a calibration technician, my role is to ensure I provide military mechanics with accurate equipment. For the future, I would like to work as a software developer. I want to serve one more year in the military and move on to working as a developer in the civilian world.
